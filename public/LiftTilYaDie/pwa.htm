PWA OLYMPIANS:

The PWA has been one of the most successful LWCs in the United States for many years. Our Coaches and Administrators have been responsible for starting and supporting many local weightlifters. The talents of our organization are so highly respected that A thletes from all parts of the Country have moved to our LWC to further their lifting careers. There are also some Athletes that have been trained by PWA members, but were unable to relocate to the West Coast.
