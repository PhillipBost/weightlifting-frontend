DRIVING DIRECTIONS


MIDTOWN STRENGTH & CONDITIONING - 2010 3rd ST, SACRAMENTO

    FROM SAN FRANCISCO & SOUTH
  • Take I-80 E. toward OAKLAND (BAY BRIDGE).
  • Continue on I-80 E. toward SACRAMENTO.
  • Keep LEFT to take US-50 E. toward SACRAMENTO/SOUTH LAKE TAHOE.
  • Take the 5TH STREET EXIT.
  • Turn SHARP LEFT onto 5TH ST.
  • Turn LEFT onto W ST.
  • Turn RIGHT onto 3RD ST.
  • End at MIDTOWN STRENGTH & CONDITIONING - 2010 3rd ST, SACRAMENTO, CA.

    FROM MARIN
  • Take US-101 N. toward EUREKA.
  • Merge onto CA-37 E via EXIT 460A toward NAPA/VALLEJO.
  • Merge onto I-80 E via the EXIT on the LEFT.
  • Keep LEFT to take US-50 E toward SACRAMENTO/SOUTH LAKE TAHOE.
  • Take the 5TH STREET EXIT.
  • Turn SHARP LEFT onto 5TH ST.
  • Turn LEFT onto W ST.
  • Turn RIGHT onto 3RD ST.
  • End at MIDTOWN STRENGTH & CONDITIONING - 2010 3rd ST, SACRAMENTO, CA.

    FROM RENO & NORTH
  • Take I-80 W (Crossing into CALIFORNIA).
  • Take the I-80-BL / CAPITAL CITY FWY EXIT- EXIT 95- toward CA-99 S/SACRAMENTO.
  • Merge onto US-50 W/I-80-BR W/CA-99 N toward I-5/SAN FRANCISCO.
  • Take the CA-99 N/I-5 N EXIT toward REDDING.
  • Take the Q STREET EXIT.
  • Turn SLIGHT RIGHT onto Q ST.
  • Turn RIGHT onto 3RD ST.
  • End at MIDTOWN STRENGTH & CONDITIONING - 2010 3rd ST, SACRAMENTO, CA.
