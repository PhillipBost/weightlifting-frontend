REDWOOD EMPIRE CHAMPIONSHIPS, COTATI
DRIVING DIRECTIONS


LA PLAZA PARK - OLD REDWOOD HWY/W. SIERRA AVE.

    From South of Cotati:
  • Take U.S. 101 North
  • Exit at the Cotati West Sierra Ave. exit (Gravenstein HWY).
  • Turn right onto West Sierra,
  • Continue about 1/2 mile to La Plaza Park on the left.

    From North of Cotati:
  • Take U.S. 101 South
  • to the 116 exit (Cotati, Sebastopol).
  • Make a left at the light and go under the freeway.
  • Turn right onto Old Redwood Hwy. at the second light
  • Continue about a mile to La Plaza Park on the right
